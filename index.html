ULTIMATE DINING EXPERIENCE LIKE NO OTHER

Welcome to Afghan Famous Kabob restaurant in Gainesville, Virginia. Opened in 2006, this little jewel offers a selection of famous traditional Afghan dishes in the area. The stars of the menu are the kabobs – there are 10 choices – each cooked to order over charcoal. Choices include boneless chicken thighs, boneless chicken breast, bone-in chicken, seasoned ground beef, chunks of lamb and lamb ribs
If you are thinking about having a private party, we can definitely cater to your specific needs by offering full service catering.

Food Critics Love Us!

Our fresh yogurt made from scratch in house and our hot bread brought to you right out of the tanoor, a traditional stone oven, brings in awards every year from the Washingtonian Cheap Eats edition, local lifestyle magazines and rave reviews on yelp! Come in and see what all the fuss is about!
